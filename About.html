A B O U T

Hey, I’m Nizar – a passionate and curious graphic designer based in Pattambi.

I recently completed a diploma in multimedia animation and have been building my skills through personal projects, internships, and freelance work. I love exploring how design can solve problems, tell stories, and make everyday experiences a little more beautiful.

My style leans toward clean, modern, and minimal, but I’m always experimenting and learning. Whether it’s branding, social media graphics, or poster design, I enjoy bringing creative ideas to life and making visuals that connect.

I’m currently looking for exciting opportunities to grow, collaborate, and keep learning from talented creatives and teams.
